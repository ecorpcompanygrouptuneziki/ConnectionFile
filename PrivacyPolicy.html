Tuneziki Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Tuneziki may collect the following information when you sign up for our services or use the app:

  • Your name and contact details (email address, phone number).
  • Your artist or band name, profile information, and music tracks.
  • Payment information, such as credit card details or other payment method details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve Tuneziki's music distribution services.
  • Personalize your experience with Tuneziki, such as recommending relevant music or artists.
  • Process payments and offer customer support.
  • Analyze usage patterns, track app performance, and make data-driven improvements to Tuneziki.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party services, such as streaming platforms, to distribute your music and provide additional promotional opportunities.
  • Trusted third-party service providers who help us operate, improve, and secure Tuneziki.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
  • If required by law or if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or comply with a legal process.
